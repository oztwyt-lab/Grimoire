PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

Grimoire is a recipe management app developed by Dagger Studios. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following information when you create an account and use the app:

— Email address (used for account creation and login)

— Recipe data you create (names, ingredients, preparation steps)

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is used solely to provide the app's functionality — storing and retrieving your recipes. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud infrastructure provided by Google LLC. Data is stored on Google's servers and subject to Google's security practices.

DATA DELETION

You can delete your recipes at any time from within the app. To request full account and data deletion, contact us at the email below.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Grimoire is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

CONTACT
